# Frame.ego

**Frame.ego** is a "boutique" Chrome extension designed for creative professionals who need a little extra love. It injects a curated stream of high-fidelity, positive reinforcement comments into your **Frame.io** review pages (V3 and V4 supported).

Turn "Can you make it pop?" into *"This is absolute genius!"* with a single click.

---

## Features

- **Dual-Version Support**: Seamlessly works with both Frame.io V3 and the new V4 "Vapor" interface.
- **Ego-Boosting Comments**: Injects extra, high-fidelity positive reinforcement comments into your review sidebar. It **never** modifies or deletes existing project comments.
- **Seamless Integration**: Injected comments use project-appropriate names and avatars to blend naturally into the UI without replacing any real client feedback.
- **Boutique Control Center**:
    - **Stroke Ego**: Toggle the extension on/off instantly.
    - **Hype Level**: Adjust from "Mild" to "Unhinged" gaslighting.
    - **Fanbase Size**: Control the volume of praise (Sprinkle, Plenty, or Spam).
- **Premium UI**: A sleek, dark-mode popup with brand-teal gradients, interactive tooltips, and clickable slider labels.
- **Zero Performance Impact**: Light-weight content scripts and efficient mutation observers ensure your review pages stay fast.

## Installation

1. **Download** or **Clone** this repository to your local machine.
2. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Enable **Developer mode** (toggle in the top right).
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the root directory of the `FrameEgo` folder.
5. Pin **Frame.ego** to your toolbar for quick access.

## Usage

1. Navigate to any Frame.io review link (`app.frame.io` or `next.frame.io`).
2. Click the **F** icon in your toolbar.
3. Configure your desired **Hype Level** and **Fanbase Size**.
4. Click **STROKE MY EGO**.
5. Watch the sidebar fill with the praise you deserve.
